Army Baylor MHA/MBA Coin Tournament (AY2026)
Welcome to the official voting dashboard for the Army Baylor Class of 2026–2028 Coin Tournament!

How to Participate
Enter your name: Type your name in the "Current Voter Name" field to unlock the bracket.

Cast your votes: Click the Vote buttons to support your favorite designs in each round.

Hover over any coin image to zoom in and inspect the design details!

Track progress: Watch the "Weighted Leaderboard" update in real-time as the tournament progresses.

Submit: Once the Championship round is decided, click the "📤 Submit Final Results to Sheets" button to lock in your class-wide decision.

Tournament Rules
Weighted Scoring: Every match counts differently as we move through the bracket to ensure the best design emerges as the winner.

Play-In: 1x Multiplier

Quarterfinals: 2x Multiplier

Semifinals: 3x Multiplier

Championship: 5x Multiplier

Fair Play: Votes are tracked by name to ensure one-person-one-vote fairness.

Design Contenders
This tournament features 10 unique designs submitted by our class, ranging from AI-themed "CyBEAR" concepts to traditional Baylor heritage designs.
